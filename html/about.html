About Me

Growing up, I immersed myself in just about every type of game imaginableâ€”board games, sports, traditional folk games, card games, arcade games, Nintendo classics, PlayStation adventures, Xbox titles, and PC games. If it had the word "game" attached to it, I was all in. These experiences didn't just entertain me; they shaped how I think about creativity, challenge, and the joy of play.

From a young age, I've always loved diving into new worlds and exploring different types of gameplay, and those experiences have sparked my passion for creating my own. Even in elementary school, I'd sketch out game ideas on notebook paper, creating games that my classmates and I could enjoy together, confined to our own little world within the classroom.

Now, I channel that lifelong love for games into crafting my own. It's no longer just about chasing fame or fortune, but about the joy of creating something that's fun, immersive, and meaningful. For me, game development is a boundless journey, and I'm excited to see where it takes me next.
