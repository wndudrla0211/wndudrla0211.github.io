About This Game

"Immature Love" is an interactive story game that explores the complexities of real relationships and personal growth. It depicts a deep journey through trust issues, jealousy, and personal development experienced in a relationship with an ex-girlfriend.

This visual novel game honestly portrays the immature aspects of love and the process of growth, allowing players to reflect on their own relationships and gain insights for healthier love.

Development Notes

This game was created based on real personal experiences. By honestly portraying immature behaviors without glorification and the growth process, we hope players can reflect on their own relationships and gain insights for healthier love.

The game emphasizes that love is not just about emotions, but also about personal growth, trust-building, and the maturity to handle relationships responsibly.
