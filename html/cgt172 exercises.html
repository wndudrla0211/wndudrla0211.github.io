About This Course

Through CGT 172, I learned the fundamental principles of UX design and user-centered design processes. Through weekly assignments, I practiced and understood various UX methodologies including user research, wireframing, prototyping, and usability testing. I completed projects that implement essential UX design principles and created user-friendly interfaces as required by the course. Through this process, I was able to understand the importance of user experience and learn the fundamental aspects of effective interface design. Exercises and Projects include User Research Methods, Wireframing Techniques, Interactive Prototyping, Usability Testing, Information Architecture, Visual Design Principles, User Interface Design Patterns.
