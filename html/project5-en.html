About This Game

"Immature Love" is an interactive story game that explores the complexities of real relationships and personal growth. It depicts a deep journey through trust issues, jealousy, and personal development experienced in a relationship with an ex-girlfriend.

This visual novel game honestly portrays the immature aspects of love and the process of growth, allowing players to reflect on their own relationships and gain insights for healthier love.

Game Features

🎭 Realistic Story
• Real relationship story based on actual experiences
• Honestly portrayed immaturity including anger and harsh words
• Inferiority complex and jealousy from lack of ability and confidence
• Relationship conflicts when not ready for love
🎮 Game System
• Various story branches based on player choices
• Manage trust with seoeun, jealousy toward Minsu, and self-confidence
• 25 different endings
• Save/Load system (3 slots)
💔 Main Themes
• Growth from immature love to mature love
• Overcoming trust issues and jealousy
• Self-reflection and personal growth
• The meaning of genuine apology and reconciliation
📱 Technical Features
• Responsive design supporting both PC and mobile
• Typing animations and smooth UI transitions
• Game progress saving using localStorage
• Touch event optimization for perfect mobile controls

Story Characters

👨 Protagonist - An immature person with the will to grow
👩 Seoeun (Ex-girlfriend) - A realistic woman who values trust
👨 Minsu (Seoeun's Ex-boyfriend) - Seoeun's friend who becomes an object of jealousy

Development Notes

This game was created based on real personal experiences. By honestly portraying immature behaviors without glorification and the growth process, we hope players can reflect on their own relationships and gain insights for healthier love.

The game emphasizes that love is not just about emotions, but also about personal growth, trust-building, and the maturity to handle relationships responsibly.
